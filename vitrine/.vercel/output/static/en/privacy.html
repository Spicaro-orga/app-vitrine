Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

SPICARO, located at 5 RUE LOUIS GUESPIN 92140 Clamart, is committed to protecting the personal data of its users in accordance with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the French Data Protection Act ("Loi Informatique et Libertés"). This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Data Collected

We collect the following data:

  • Identification Information: Name, first name, email address, phone number.
  • Professional Information: Position, company, industry sector.
  • Connection Information: IP address, browser type, browsing history.
  • Financial Information: Billing and payment data, if applicable.

3. Use of Data

Your personal data is used in the following cases:

  • Customer Relationship Management: To respond to your requests, manage your orders or services.
  • Marketing: To send you information about our services, offers, or events, subject to your consent.
  • Improvement of Our Services: To personalize your experience on our site and improve our services.
  • Legal Compliance: To fulfill our legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with service providers acting on our behalf, such as payment or hosting services.
  • Legal Requirements: In response to a legal or regulatory obligation, or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or disclosure.

6. Retention Period

We retain your personal data as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7. Your Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data, unless retention is necessary for legal reasons.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data for legitimate reasons.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request to receive your data in a structured and commonly used format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@spicaro.fr.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
